XCOMBAT FOR WINDOWS - KEY COMMANDS (AS OF SEPTEMBER 02, 2003)
-------------------------------------------------------------

GENERAL
-------
ESC             Exit XCombat        

DISPLAY
-------
A, a            Toggles Axis
R               Toggles Search Radar for Current Aircraft
r               Toggles Instantaneous Radar for Current Aircraft
J, j            Toggle Jamming displays
D, d            Toggle RWR detection display
T, t            Toggles ground and sky texture
O, o            Enables missile and object/aircraft traces
H, h            Toggles HUD when in cockpit mode
B, b            Toggles backshading on GUI
L, l		Toggles labels

VIEWS
-----
M, m            Toggles Basic Models
S, s            Selects Spotter View
C, c            Selects Cockpit View
W, w            Selects Worldview
V, v            Selects Viewpoint
I, i            Selects Missile View
N, n            Select next missile to view (if available for current a/c)



ROTATIONS
---------
X/x             Rotate around x-axis +ve and -ve in 10 deg. increments
Y/y             Rotate around y-axis +ve and -ve in 10 deg. increments
Z/z             Rotate around z-axis +ve and -ve in 10 deg. increments


ZOOM
----
[               Zoom in
]               Zoom out


SELECTING AIRCAFT
-----------------
PGDOWN          Next aircraft
PGUP            Previous aircraft

TIME STEPPING
-------------
G, g            Go/No. Toggles moving/animation
LEFT ARROW      Time decrement by 0.1 seconds
RIGHT ARROW     Time increment by 0.1 seconds
UP ARROW        Time increment by 10 seconds
DOWN ARROW      Time decrement by 10 seconds

FUNCTION KEYS
-------------
F1              Toggle Radar Display
F2              Toggle Info Box
F3              Toggle Real Time Sync (this doesn't work properly yet)
F4              Toggle Waypoints 1
F5              Toggle Waypoints 2
F6              Toggle Bodyroll
F7		Toggle Fullscreen
F8		Toggle Radar Shading
